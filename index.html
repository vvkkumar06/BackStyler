Back Styler

Description: Changes the style of the Body Background

Author: Vivek Kumar

Features

1> Change color of your background of your website dynamically, Choose a color out of 24 colors.

2> Choose Textures out of 24 Textures and use it as your website background dynamically.

3> Good looking and easy to understand graphical user interface

How to use

Download the plugin and unzip ,copy the "Back-Styler" folder in your website folder then add the following lines of code within head section:

<script type="text/javascript" src="Back-Styler/jquery.js" ></script> <script type="text/javascript" src="Back-Styler/myscript.js" ></script>

Enjoy!
